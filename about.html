About Asiris

Asiris, whose real name is Luke Simpson, is a rising artist in the contemporary pop and gospel scene. Born and raised in Texas, he began composing music at the age of thirteen and has been driven by his passion for both music and faith. Though he does not exclusively label his work as "Christian music," his lyrics often incorporate his spiritual journey and belief in God's guidance.

His breakthrough came with the hit single "my friend's toyota," which resonated with millions of listeners. Luke continues to balance his career with studies in business and marketing, showcasing his dedication both as an artist and a student.
